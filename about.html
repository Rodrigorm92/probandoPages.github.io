Northside Your Soccer League



About

Misión

Support young athletes who live in North Chicago and have interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to teamwork and healthy competition.

Visión

The Northside Your Soccer League aspires to train great athletes through the development of personality, discipline and leadership.

Misión

The Northside Your Soccer League was founded in 1996 for an environment in where to learn to play soccer for north chicago athletes, to be A member of the NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 and 12 and live in North Chicago. NYSL is run by a small, full-time team that relies on generous volunteers such as parents and former league members
